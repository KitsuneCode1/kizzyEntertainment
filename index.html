Kizzy Entertainment Management

Meet the Management

CEO (KizzyCodes)

Behind every groundbreaking movement is a visionary who sees the code of the future. For Kizzy Entertainment, that visionary is KizzyCodes, the Founder.

KizzyCodes has been the primary architect of its mission, blending a modern, tech-forward mindset with a passion for live, high-energy entertainment. As the Founder, KizzyCodes is the source of the brand's core DNA. The name itself suggests a bridge between the digital and the physical—the idea that entertainment can be "coded" for perfection while remaining raw and authentic.

Manager (Karen)

Karen’s role is the most critical balancing act in the company. She sits at the center of the brand, managing the intersection of talent, logistics, and business growth. Her leadership style is defined by precision, diplomacy, and an unbreakable focus on the big picture.

While the creative's dream and the coordinators strategize, Karen is the one who ensures the contracts are signed, the deadlines are met, and the Kizzy Entertainment standard is never compromised.

Head of Media (Ecstassy Graphics)

Ecstassy Graphics, a multifaceted creative powerhouse within the Kizzy Entertainment team.

Serving as both the Head of Media and the Lead Designer, Ecstassy Graphics is the architect behind the brand’s visual identity. However, her contribution goes beyond the digital canvas; as a professional model, she also represent the physical embodiment of the brand's style and energy.

Brand Coordinator (Risk Taker)

As the Brand Coordinator, Risk Taker is the bridge between the creative vision of the media team and the logistical reality of event hosting. He ensures that "Kizzy Entertainment" identity remains consistent, daring, and ahead of the curve.
